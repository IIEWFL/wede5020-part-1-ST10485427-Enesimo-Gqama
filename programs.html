Our Programs

Discover how we provide integrated care and education to the children we serve.

The Babies Home

Our Babies Home is more than just a home: it is a family. We provide 24-hour care for abandoned, orphaned and vulnerable children from newborn to five years old. Our approach is based on providing a caring, family-style environment in which each child receives personalised love, attention and developmental assistance.

  • 24/7 residental care in a family-unit style
  • Nutritional meals and health monitoring
  • Early childhood stimulation and play-based learning
  • Preparation for reconciliation into family or adoption

Preschool

Our Early Childhood Development (ECD) centre delivers high-quality education to both Babies Home and children from the nearby neighbourhood. We follow a structured curriculum designed to prepare children for formal schooling with an emphasis on literacy, numeracy, social skills and creative expression.

  • Qualified ECD practitioners
  • Structured curriculum aligned with national holidays
  • Safe and stimulating learning environment
  • Nutritious meals for all students
