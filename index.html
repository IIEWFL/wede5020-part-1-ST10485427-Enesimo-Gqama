Providing Love, Safety and Education

Botshabelo is a place of refuge for vulnerable children in Midrand, offering a loving home and quality early childhood development.

Our Programs

  • Babies Home

    • We offer a safe and loving home environment for abandoned, orphaned, and vulnerable infants and toddlers.

  • Preschool

    • Our Early Childhood Development (ECD) center provides exceptional early childhood education to prepare children for a successful future.

    Our Impact

    <
  • Babies Home:

    • Since launching in 2000, we have cared for over 220 children, with over 200 being adopted or reunited with their family.

  • Urban Kids Educentre:

    • Since 2011, we have grown from 7 students to over 120 in 6 classrooms, serving over 600 children.

  • Uplifted Teacher Training:

    • We empowered over 150 childhood development practitioners in underprivileged community preschool centres in previous years and are working with over 100 more in the current year.
